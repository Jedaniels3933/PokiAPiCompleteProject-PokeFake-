    Mewtwo

    MewTwo was first introduced in the Pokémon Red and Blue games as a clone of the mythical Pokémon Mew. Created by scientists, MewTwo is known for its immense psychic powers and its desire to prove its superiority over other Pokémon.

    Weird Stuff

    The weird stuff is a collection of strange and mysterious Pokémon that defy classification. From the ghostly Gengar to the enigmatic Unown, these Pokémon are shrouded in mystery and intrigue, adding an element of the unknown to the Pokémon world.

    Badook

    The Badook is a legendary Pokémon that is said to bring good luck and fortune to those who encounter it. Its mystical powers are said to grant wishes and fulfill dreams, making it a highly sought-after Pokémon by trainers and collectors alike.

    Pokemon Trainers

    The life of a pokemon trainer is a life of adventure and excitement. Trainers travel the world, catching and training Pokémon to compete in battles and tournaments. They form strong bonds with their Pokémon, working together to overcome challenges and achieve their goals.

    Team Trouble

    The pokemon stealing Team Trouble is a group of villains who seek to exploit Pokémon for their own gain. Led by the enigmatic Giovanni, Team Trouble is known for their ruthless tactics and their desire to control the world through their Pokémon. Trainers must be wary of their schemes and work together to stop them.

    This is for children?

    Pokemon is not just a kids brand anymore. It has grown into a global phenomenon, with fans of all ages enjoying the games, trading cards, animated series, and movies. The franchise continues to evolve and expand, captivating audiences around the world with its rich lore and engaging characters.

    About Pokémon

    Pokemon was created by Satoshi Tajiri and Ken Sugimori in 1995. It has since become a global phenomenon, with games, trading cards, an animated series, and movies. The franchise continues to evolve and expand, captivating fans of all ages.
